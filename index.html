What is MNANP2025?

MNANP2025 is a workshop on Modeling and Numerical Analysis of Nonlinear Phenomena that will explore the latest research and developments in this field. Focusing on practical applications, the workshop will cover topics like nonlinear partial differential equations, dynamical systems, and optimization methods. Leading experts will share their findings, and interactive sessions will encourage collaboration and knowledge exchange. Attendees will gain insights into mathematical frameworks and computational tools for addressing complex problems in physics, biology, engineering, and more.
